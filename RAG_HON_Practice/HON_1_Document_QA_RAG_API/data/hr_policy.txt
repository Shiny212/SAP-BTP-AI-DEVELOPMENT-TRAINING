HR Policy Document

Annual Leave Policy:
Employees are entitled to 18 days of annual leave per year.

Sick Leave Policy:
Employees can take 10 sick leaves per year.

Work From Home Policy:
Employees can work from home up to 2 days per week with manager approval.

Carry Forward Policy:
Employees can carry forward up to 10 unused annual leave days.

Notice Period:
The notice period for resignation is 60 days.
