HR Policy Document


Annual Leave Policy:

Employees are entitled to 18 days of annual leave per year.

Unused annual leave can be carried forward.
Employees can carry forward up to 10 unused annual leave days.


Sick Leave Policy:

Employees receive 10 sick leave days every year.


Maternity Leave Policy:

Employees are eligible for 26 weeks of maternity leave.


Adoption Leave Policy:

Adoption leave is available for eligible employees according to company policy.
