=========================================
SMARTKART CUSTOMER SUPPORT KNOWLEDGE BASE
=========================================

Refund Policy
-------------
Customers can request a refund within 7 days of product delivery.

Products must be unused and returned in their original packaging.

Approved refunds are processed within 5 to 7 business days.

Refunds are credited to the customer's original payment method.

Refund requests after 7 days are generally not accepted unless the product is damaged or defective.


Shipping Policy
---------------
Standard Delivery:
3 to 5 business days.

Express Delivery:
1 to 2 business days.

Same Day Delivery:
Available only in selected cities.

Orders above ₹2,000 qualify for free standard delivery.

Customers receive a tracking ID once the order is shipped.


Delivery Charges
----------------
Premium Customers:
Free delivery on all orders.

Standard Customers:
Free delivery for orders above ₹2,000.

Orders below ₹2,000 incur a delivery charge of ₹100.


Order Cancellation Policy
-------------------------
Orders can be cancelled before they are shipped.

Orders that are already shipped cannot be cancelled.

Customers can initiate cancellation from the SmartKart application or contact customer support.


Return Policy
-------------
Products can be returned within 7 days of delivery.

Returned items must be unused and include all original accessories.

Damaged or incorrect products are eligible for free return pickup.


Account Policy
--------------
Customers can update their personal details through the SmartKart account portal.

Inactive accounts can be reactivated by contacting customer support.

Customers can permanently delete their account by submitting a request to customer support.


Premium Membership
------------------
Premium members receive:

• Free delivery
• Priority customer support
• Early access to promotional offers
• Exclusive member discounts


Support Policy
--------------
Premium customers receive 24×7 chat and email support.

Standard customers receive support from Monday to Saturday between 9:00 AM and 6:00 PM.

Critical issues are given higher priority.


Payment Policy
--------------
SmartKart supports:

• UPI
• Credit Card
• Debit Card
• Net Banking
• Wallet Payments
• Cash on Delivery (Selected Locations)

Payment failures are usually reversed within 3 to 5 business days.


Privacy Policy
--------------
Customer information is encrypted and securely stored.

SmartKart never shares customer information with third parties without consent.


Frequently Asked Questions
--------------------------

Q: How can I track my order?

Use the tracking link available in the Orders section of the SmartKart application.

Q: When will I receive my refund?

Approved refunds are processed within 5 to 7 business days.

Q: How do I become a Premium customer?

Premium membership can be purchased from the Membership section inside the SmartKart application.

Q: Can I change my delivery address?

The delivery address can only be changed before the order is shipped.

Q: Can I return a damaged product?

Yes. Damaged products are eligible for return and replacement.

Q: How do I contact customer support?

You can reach SmartKart support through live chat, email, or the customer care number available in the application.
